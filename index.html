The Incredible Banana Bread !

The actual tutorial for the Banana Bread

Ingredients Amount
Flour 2 cups
Baking Powder 2 teaspoons
Butter ¾ cup
Sugar ¾ cup
Eggs 2
Vanilla To taste
Bananas 3
Chocolate Chips To taste
Coconut Milk ½ cup

Kitchen Utensils

Utensil Quantity
Oven 1
Bowl 2
Cake Mould 1
Spatula 1
spoon 1
whisk 1
measuring cup 1
  • First, preheat your oven to 350° Fahrenheit.
  • Put two cups of flour in a bowl and add two teaspoons of baking powder.
  • In a separate bowl, combine ¾ cup of butter and ¾ cup of sugar, and mix them together.
  • Add two eggs to the mixture to make the banana bread bigger.
  • Add some vanilla, three bananas, and MIX MIX MIX.
  • Add chocolate chips and ½ cup of coconut milk. Then, combine this mixture with the previously prepared mixture of butter, sugar, banana and vanilla.
  • Next, add the contents of the first bowl.
  • Pour your mixture into a mold and place it in the oven for 30 minutes. Check your bread every 20 to 10 minutes. Then, BONNE APPÉTIT !

The BEST Chicken Burger

The actual tutorial for the Chicken Burger

Ingredient Amount
Burger Buns 2
Mayonaise A little bit
Paties 2
Cheese 2 slices
Tomatoes 2 slices

Kitchen Ustensil

Utensil Description
Toaster A toaster for cooking the buns.
Oven You need an oven for cooking the paties
Sharp Knife Cut the tomatoes
Spatula For flip the paties
Heat Resistant Gloves For taking the paties out of the oven
  • First, preheat your oven to 450°F. Then, toast your buns in the toaster for 5 minutes.
  • Next, you will take your patties and place them in the oven for 20 minutes. Remember to check them every 10 minutes and flip them around.
  • Apply some mayonnaise on your buns, then add two slices of cheese and a little bit of tomato.
  • You will flip the patties and let them cook for another 10 minutes. After the patties are done cooking, be careful not to get burned. I recommend using heat-resistant gloves to handle them.
  • Place your patties in the burger, then close it.
  • Then BONNE APPÉTIT !
